HOW IT WORKS!

In simple terms, Multimodal Sentimental Analysis works in three stages

DIFFUSION

'DiffusionStage' is a class in our system that handles the processing of uploaded videos to extract and segregate different modalities. It enables the visualization of different modalities and sentiments for better understanding and forms the basis for further analysis and processing in our system.

MODEL EXECUTION STAGE

Here, the extracted modalities are given as input to 3 different models. These models use machine learning, neural networks, and data science concepts to provide accurate results. Each of our models has an accuracy of more than 80%.

FUSION STAGE

Fusion stage, as the name suggests, combines the results. It uses mathematical statistics to obtain the final result.
